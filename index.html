• The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill

    Aquarius

    Released in 1998, The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill progressively blended neo-soul, R&B, and hip-hop seamlessly, resulting in one of the most acclaimed albums of all time. An Aquarius is known for being very emotionally open, as well as original. Lauryn Hill’s album has these traits on display throughout its entire tracklist.

  • Blonde

    Pisces

    Pisces are known for being compassionate, artistic, musical, and overly trusting. Frank Ocean’s sophomore album, Blonde, is an exploration of the successful and unsuccessful relationships that Ocean has experienced in his lifetime. Considered to be one of the greatest albums of the past decade, there is no better choice to represent Pisces than Blonde.

  • My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

    Aries

    Confident, determined, honest, impulsive: all traits used to describe an Aries. Kanye West’s, My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy includes these traits in spades. Whether it's the boastful anthem, POWER, or the introspective, Runaway, Kanye exemplifies these traits throughout its 13 song tracklist.

  • The Blueprint

    Taurus

    Despite being his sixth studio album, The Blueprint is often considered to be Jay-Z’s best. This type of patience and devotion to the craft of improving could only be represented by a Taurus.
