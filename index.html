The Miseducation Of Lauryn Hill

Released in 1998, The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill progressively blended neo-soul, R&B, and hip-hop seamlessly, resulting in one of the most acclaimed albums of all time.

Blonde

Blonde is the second studio album by American singer Frank Ocean. It was released on August 20, 2016, as a timed exclusive on the iTunes Store and Apple Music.

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy is the fifth studio album by Kanye West. It was released on November 22, 2010, by Def Jam Recordings and Roc-A-Fella Records.

The Blueprint

The Blueprint is the sixth studio album by American rapper Jay-Z. It was released on September 11, 2001, by Roc-A-Fella Records and Def Jam Recordings.

Telefone

Telefone is the debut mixtape by American rapper Noname. Originally announced in 2012, the mixtape faced numerous delays and was finally released four years later on July 31, 2016 as a free digital download.

Take Care

Take Care is the second studio album by Canadian rapper Drake. It was released on November 15, 2011 by Young Money Entertainment, Cash Money Records and Universal Republic Records.

Stankonia

Stankonia is the fourth studio album by American hip hop duo OutKast. It was released on October 31, 2000, by LaFace Records.

Swimming

Swimming is the fifth studio album by American rapper and singer Mac Miller and his last album to be released during his lifetime. It was released on August 3, 2018 by REMember Music and Warner Bros. Records.

Under Pressure

Under Pressure is the debut studio album by American rapper Logic. It was released on October 21, 2014, by Visionary Music Group and Def Jam Recordings.

Monster

Monster is the thirteenth mixtape by American rapper Future. It was released on October 28, 2014 by Freebandz Entertainment.

Eternal Atake

Eternal Atake is the second studio album by American rapper Lil Uzi Vert. After being initially announced in 2018, the album was released on March 6, 2020, through Generation Now and Atlantic Records as a surprise release.

To Pimp A Butterfly

To Pimp a Butterfly is the third studio album by American rapper Kendrick Lamar. It was released on March 15, 2015, by Top Dawg Entertainment, Aftermath Entertainment and Interscope Records.
